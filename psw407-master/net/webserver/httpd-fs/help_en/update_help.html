Update Firmware

Firmware update instruction:


  1. 1. Click the "Browse" button and specify the path to the firmware file: PSWupdate***.img, where *** is the firmware version
  2. 2. Click "Upload" button and wait until the file uploads
  3. 3. Click the "Update" button (switch firmware update) and the update process will begin


Warning! Do not turn off the power during the update process

New firmware versions are available on the websitewww.tfortis.com
