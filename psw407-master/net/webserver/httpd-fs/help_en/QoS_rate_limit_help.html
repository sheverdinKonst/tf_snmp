Port Bandwidth

A configurable limit on the maximum receive and transmit rate for each port is available.

RX limit - limit of incoming traffic in Kbps
TX limit - limit of outgoing traffic in Kbps

Note 1: If the port operates without limit, set the RX limit and TX limit fields to 0

Note 2: the speed limit is available in the range from 64 Kbps to 102400 Kbps for Fast Ethernet ports and up to 256000 Kbps for Gigabit Ethernet ports
