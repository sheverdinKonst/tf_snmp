Rate Limit

Restriction of broadcast and multicast distribution, as well as distribution of an unknown unicast is intended to prevent the occurrence of a broadcast storm.

Unknown Unicast Rate Limit - estriction of unicast unknown MAC destination address. By default, an unknown unicast is broadcast to all ports.
Broadcast Rate Limit - restriction of incoming broadcast traffic.
Multicast Rate Limit - restriction of the incoming multicast.

Rate Limit Threshold - speed limit in percentage
